Frequently Asked Questions
How do I install a new songbook?
+
Go to the Songbooks tab at the bottom of the screen and tap the Available tab. You will see all songbooks ready to install. Tap on one to download it to your device.
How do I transpose a song to a different key?
+
Open a song and tap the settings icon in the top right corner. In the chord panel, use the minus and plus buttons next to Transpose to shift the key up or down. The chords update instantly.
Can I switch between letter and solfege notation?
+
Yes. Open a song in chord view and tap the notation selector next to Chord Notation. You can toggle between letter notation (A, B, C) and solfege notation (Do, Re, Mi) at any time.
How do I see chord diagrams for guitar or ukulele?
+
In chord view, tap Guitar or Ukulele next to Chords by Instrument. Then tap any chord name displayed above the lyrics to see the full fingering diagram for that chord.
How do I add a song to my favorites?
+
Open the song and tap the heart icon labeled Favorite in the chord or settings panel. The song will appear in your Favorites section on the Home screen for quick access.
How do I change the text size?
+
Go to the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen. Under Display, tap the Text Size dropdown and choose from Small, Medium, or Large to adjust the lyrics text size across the app.
How do I keep the screen on during a service?
+
Go to Settings and enable the Keep Screen On toggle under Display. This will prevent your device from sleeping while you are using the app.
Is the app available on Android?
+
Yes. Songbooks of Praise is available on both iOS and Android. You can download it from the Apple App Store or the Google Play Store for free.
